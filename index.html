Who we are

An experienced team of founders, with in total 90+ years in e-commerce is accompanied by a renowned team of advisors, e.g. Lennard Grewe, founder & shareholder of several digital commerce business models / founding partner of eccelerate GmbH and Coen Duetz, CEO of Timpenhof Invest GmbH.

What we do

We're building scalable cloud-based digital commerce experiences.

Wundercurves is a modern e-commerce platform for fashion in plus sizes, an overall $64bn market. We are providing a superior shopping experience for plus-size products of the most popular brands with a 15x greater selection than leading plus-size retailer.

Cunnicola is our e-commerce platform for premium lifestyle products and services. The sites broad assortment of solely high-quality products across multiple categories is topped by an inspiring online magazine — jointly celebrating a sophisticated lifestyle.
